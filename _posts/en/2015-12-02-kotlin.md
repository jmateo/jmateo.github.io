As a Java developer, besides the weather, our daily code can bring about fatigue, loss of the joy of coding, loss of motivation, or even loss of all pleasure.

This presentation will allow you to look to the future with a positive outlook and show you that it’s possible to be a happy, efficient, and joyful Java developer.

We will cover all aspects of Kotlin and how this language can be applied as a cure, palliative treatment, or preventive measure.

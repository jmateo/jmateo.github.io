Overview of DDD with the presentation of the three key elements that make up DDD:
**An approach** driven by models, contextualized by “Bounded Contexts” and especially centered on Ubiquitous Language
**Building blocks** the famous DDD “patterns”, not the main focus but what allows you to “get into” DDD most easily.
**Design principles**
* **Tactical** : presentation of practices promoting “supple design”
* **Strategic** : the software we develop always integrates into a larger environment consisting of multiple contexts; the principles of “strategic design” help us in this integration and in how we build our complex software.

Concretely, we will then spend 1 hour diving into the building blocks and talking more specifically about Entities and Value Objects.
This dive will address the fundamental concepts of state, identity, and values. This first presentation will allow you to apply simple and effective principles to your project from the next day, promoting better control of complexity, before addressing more advanced concepts built on these basic building blocks in future meetups.

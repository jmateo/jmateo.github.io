Andréas is a software engineer at Redsen. He started tinkering with computers as a kid. Although he's primarily a programmer, he also has interests in data analysis, infrastructure, and hardware. This broad love for computer science allowed him to take on roles as a database administrator, developer, and tech lead. After more than a decade in Bordeaux, he moved to Switzerland and now enjoys views of the Alps.

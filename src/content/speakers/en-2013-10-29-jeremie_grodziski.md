Jérémie is passionate about software design and development. He has been perfecting this craft for over 25 years and loves sharing his passion.

By day, he works as a consultant at Redsen Consulting, training and supporting his clients' projects using approaches like Domain-Driven Design.

By night, he develops ZenModeler, the tool he dreams of for designing and analyzing better software. Jérémie lives in Paris and believes that curiosity is a wonderful quality!

Axel is a middleware specialist particularly sensitive to performance, concurrency, and load challenges. He worked for several years at Airbus before joining the technical leadership of Hardis, a publisher based in Grenoble. It was there that he met Dominique, who had a brief stint at Hardis in a career filled with JAVA-related IT service positions.

Dominique stayed at Hardis for only a year, but Axel and he remained in contact. When a startup idea took shape, they naturally decided to partner to launch inZair, along with Nourrédine Rouibah.

inZair was officially born on January 20, 2011, after two years of work in the evenings, weekends, and a good portion of their vacations.

In this venture, Axel took the lead on server architecture on Google App Engine, while Dominique took a more transversal role spanning from mobile client to server.

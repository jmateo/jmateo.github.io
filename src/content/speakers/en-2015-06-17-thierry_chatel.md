Thierry Chatel is an independent consultant and trainer, specialist in web application architecture and the AngularJS framework, and a member of the Google Developers Experts network since 2014.

He is deeply committed to good design and development practices that support software quality. This is why he was attracted from the start by the great qualities of the AngularJS framework, which now represents the majority of his activity.

He performs application audits, technical support, and has already led over 50 AngularJS training sessions for companies of all sizes, from small businesses to large organizations.

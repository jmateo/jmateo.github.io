Duy Hai is a technical evangelist for Datastax, the commercial company behind Apache Cassandra.

He divides his time between giving presentations/meetings/talks on Cassandra, developing open source projects for the community, and helping projects using Cassandra.

Before Datastax, Duy Hai was a freelance Java/Cassandra developer.

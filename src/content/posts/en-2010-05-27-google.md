The theme of this session will be Google technologies for enterprises and we will welcome Didier Girard, Director of Operations and Innovation at SFEIR, for this occasion. On the program you will find Google Web Toolkit, Google App Engine, Android and the latest addition, Google Wave.

This presentation will be quite practical and aims to allow everyone to go home with a better understanding of Google’s different products and how they fit into a global vision.

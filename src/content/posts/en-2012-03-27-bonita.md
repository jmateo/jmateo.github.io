Bonita Open Solution is the first complete Open Source Business Process Management (BPM) solution. Based on a robust workflow engine, it provides all the necessary tools to efficiently create business process automation applications, with all the benefits of the BPM approach and strong connectivity with existing information systems.

This presentation will first introduce the concept of BPM and its objectives, then a demonstration of Bonita Open Solution will show how it can be used to implement business applications.

By revealing quite radical changes to the framework, the announcement of the future version 2.0 of AngularJS raised many questions and concerns.

What are the timelines and planned changes? Is it still the same framework, or something completely new? Why modify it so much when it’s enjoying great success? Is it still relevant to start a project with version 1 today? How do we prepare for the arrival of version 2?

This session will be an opportunity to explain the vision behind AngularJS versions 1 and 2 and to answer questions and concerns.

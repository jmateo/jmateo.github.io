The DevOps idea is to promote collaboration and communication between software development teams and operations teams. What about putting it into practice? It mainly involves bringing administrator teams into close collaboration with R&D and QA teams.

Henri Gomez will share with you his experience and approach to implementation at eXo Platform, a software publisher.
After an introduction to DevOps principles, the following two topics will be addressed:
* Administrator / Developer Relationship: Bringing administrator teams and development teams closer together
* Continuous deployment, production procedures, incident management, configuration management, …
* Administration / QA Relationship: Using administrator experience to improve quality teams
* Taking into account operational constraints during development. Configuration management and monitoring are the two main axes.
* We will present you the tools being implemented at eXo (Jenkins / JMeter / JMXTrans / VisualVM / Graphite …)

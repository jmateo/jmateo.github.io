You develop software with the best in terms of Software Factory: agile methods, cutting-edge JS technologies, the best of development practices... In short, you’re passionate and part of the very exclusive circle of Software Crafts(wo)men?!

You believe you’re doing everything you can to deliver quality software to the Business, but you feel a certain unease. IT Operations teams look at you sideways during Go-Lives and integration phases are painful?

It’s not too late to make peace with Production!

Ansible is one of those Open Source tools stamped with the DevOps label, which make Big Vendors tremble. Acquired at the end of 2015 by Red Hat, Ansible continues its expansion in the DevOps world with complete simplicity... which is its strength!

Through several examples, you will understand the great principles of Ansible, as well as its relevance to configuration management and deployment automation issues.

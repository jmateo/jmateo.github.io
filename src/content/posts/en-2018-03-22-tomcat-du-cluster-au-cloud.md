We’ll start by looking at how to configure Tomcat to get a cluster, then we’ll examine the problems and solutions for an application in the cloud.
A cluster demonstration will be prepared and executed during the presentation and the application will be moved to the cloud. Kubernetes will be used on RPI3s for the demonstration and we’ll look at how to install it with Ansible.

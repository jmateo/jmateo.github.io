Sébastien Goasguen will present the Apache CloudStack IaaS cloud system. Launched in April, CloudStack is about to have its first Apache release: CloudStack 4.0.

This presentation will cover the steps necessary for release, the Apache Foundation process, new features specific to IaaS solutions, as well as the software structure and notably a new build system with Maven.

Sébastien will give us a demo and show us how to participate in this new Java project.

And as part of the Speaker Academy, Pablo Tamarit will give us a 15-minute Quickie on Clojure as an introduction to this evening.

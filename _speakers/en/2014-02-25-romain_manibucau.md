title: Romain Mani-Bucau

Romain has been a Java and JavaEE developer since 2010. He enjoys contributing to the improvement of open source solutions, particularly Apache products.

Romain has been actively involved in the development of Apache TomEE since early June 2011 and notably originated the creation of BatchEE and Sirona projects at Apache.

He enjoys sharing his opinions, tests, and discoveries on his blog [http://rmannibucau.wordpress.com](http://rmannibucau.wordpress.com).

You can follow him on Twitter [@rmannibucau](http://twitter.com/rmannibucau)

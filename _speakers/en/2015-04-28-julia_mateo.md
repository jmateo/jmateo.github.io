title: Julia Mateo

Julia is a consultant at Hortis. She has worked as a developer on various Java projects in Madrid, Paris, and Geneva in the banking, energy, transport, and telecommunications sectors.

She is a passionate developer highly involved in everything related to the Software Craftsmanship movement and code quality.

She is a fan of Java User Groups, Devoxx, and technology watch in general.

In 2012, she co-founded jDuchess swiss, an organization aimed at attracting and giving visibility to women in the development world.

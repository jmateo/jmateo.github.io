Sébastien Jarrin is a web developer at Hortis. He is a former backend developer who gradually migrated to the frontend for the thrill of it… and he absolutely doesn't regret it!

He worked for several years at Kelkoo and Yahoo! with Vincent Attiogbé, then moved to real estate at Drimki.

A passionate PHP enthusiast, he also develops personal projects in Symfony2 and regularly plays with JavaScript frameworks like AngularJS.
